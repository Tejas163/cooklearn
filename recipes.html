🍗 Classic Chicken Stir-Fry

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken: Mix chicken with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat wok: Add vegetable oil to a wok over high heat until smoking.
  3. Cook chicken: Add chicken in single layer. Sear for 2 minutes until golden. Remove and set aside.
  4. Cook vegetables: Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds. Add vegetables, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Combine: Return chicken to wok. Toss everything together.
  6. Serve: Enjoy hot over steamed rice!
🔪 Technique Tip: High Heat is Key!

The Maillard reaction (browning) only happens above 280°F (140°C). This creates that restaurant-style sear and nutty flavor. Don't crowd the pan!

🍝 Creamy Garlic Pasta

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 20 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta: Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté garlic: Melt butter in a pan. Add garlic, cook until fragrant (1 minute). Don't let it brown!
  3. Add cream: Pour in heavy cream. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  4. Add cheese: Remove from heat. Stir in parmesan until melted and smooth.
  5. Combine: Add pasta to the sauce. Toss well, adding pasta water if needed.
  6. Finish: Top with parsley and serve!
🔪 Technique Tip: Cheese Needs Low Heat!

Never add cheese to a hot pan! The fat in cheese will separate and become grainy if heated too quickly. Always remove from heat before adding parmesan.

🥞 Perfect Fluffy Pancakes

Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry: Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix wet: In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry. Mix until JUST combined. Lumps are okay!
  4. Rest: Let batter rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add 1/4 cup batter.
  6. Flip: Cook until bubbles form on surface (2 mins). Flip and cook 1 more minute.
  7. Serve: Stack with butter and maple syrup!
🔪 Technique Tip: Don't Overmix!

Overmixing develops gluten, making pancakes tough. Mix until just combined - lumps actually make fluffier pancakes!

🍅 Simple Tomato Pasta Sauce

Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 25 mins | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Infuse oil: Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and chili flakes. Cook until fragrant (1 minute). Don't burn!
  2. Add tomatoes: Pour in crushed tomatoes. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Simmer: Reduce heat to low. Let simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Taste: Adjust seasoning. The sauce should be thick enough to coat a spoon.
  5. Serve: Toss with your favorite pasta. Top with fresh basil!
🔪 Technique Tip: Low and Slow!

Tomato sauce develops complex flavors when simmered gently. High heat makes tomatoes taste acidic and bitter. Patience = better flavor!

🍚 Quick Vegetable Fried Rice

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 10 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 cups day-old cooked rice (cold is best!)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat wok: Add oil to wok over high heat until smoking.
  2. Scramble eggs: Pour in beaten eggs. Stir quickly until just set. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook vegetables: Add more oil if needed. Stir-fry vegetables for 2 minutes.
  4. Add rice: Break up any clumps. Add cold rice (cold rice fries better!).
  5. Season: Drizzle soy sauce around the edges of the wok. Toss to combine.
  6. Finish: Add eggs back. Drizzle sesame oil. Top with green onions.
🔪 Technique Tip: Use Cold Rice!

Freshly cooked rice has too much moisture and will become mushy. Cold rice grains are dry and separate nicely when fried. Make rice a day ahead!

🍛 Easy Chicken Curry

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 30 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Brown chicken: Heat oil in a pot. Season chicken with salt. Brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. Cook onions: In the same pot, add onions. Cook until soft and golden (5 mins).
  3. Toast spices: Add curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes: Add tomatoes. Cook until they break down (5 mins).
  5. Add chicken: Return chicken to pot. Pour in coconut milk.
  6. Simmer: Cover and simmer for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Serve: Garnish with cilantro. Enjoy with rice or naan!
🔪 Technique Tip: Toast Your Spices!

Dry roasting spices in hot oil releases their oils and intensifies flavor. This simple step makes a huge difference in curry depth!
